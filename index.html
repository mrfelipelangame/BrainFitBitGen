Learn the Soroban Methods


Simple Method

Simple = soma/subtração direta, **sem bead5 ou carry**. Ex: 1+5=6, 5+1=6, 4−2=2

Small Friend

Small Friend = operação que exige bead5. Ex: 3+2=5 usando bead5

Big Friend

Big Friend = operação que exige carry/borrow. Ex: 7+6=13

Mixed Method

Mixed = combina Small Friend e Big Friend. Ex: 7+6=13 usando 10−4
