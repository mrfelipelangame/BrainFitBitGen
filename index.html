Learn the Soroban Methods


Simple Method

The Simple Method means the numbers can be added or subtracted directly on the soroban without special techniques.

Example:

3 + 1 = 4

Small Friend

This method is used when the lower beads are not enough to complete the operation.

Example:

4 + 3

Add 5 and subtract 2.

4 + 5 − 2 = 7

Big Friend

This method is used when the operation requires borrowing or carrying with 10.

Example:

8 + 7

Add 10 and subtract 3.

8 + 10 − 3 = 15

Mixed Method

The Mixed method combines Small Friend and Big Friend techniques.

Example:

7 + 6

Add 10 and subtract 4.

7 + 10 − 4 = 13
